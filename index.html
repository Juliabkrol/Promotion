About The Artist

Llew Mejia is a stylistic illustrator skilled in hand-drawn illustration, pattern/print, packaging, branding, and computer generated illustration. In his younger years, his family traveled back and forth between Mexico and the Southwestern United States. As a result he is bilingual and has a unique perspective on American as well as Mexican culture. Llew enjoys working on patterns for print, lettering, painting, buying folk art, watering his plants, and perusing through old occult books.
